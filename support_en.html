Support for Sjekkliste_Y

This page provides help and information if something in the Sjekkliste_Y app is not working as expected.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the app for?

Sjekkliste_Y is designed for private individuals who are taking delivery of a Tesla Model Y and want a structured way to inspect the vehicle.

Do I need an internet connection to use the app?

No. The checklist works fully offline. An internet connection is only required if you choose to share or store the generated PDF report using external services.

What data does the app collect?

The app does not collect or transmit any personal data. All notes, images and checklist results are stored locally on your device.

Where are photos and reports stored?

Photos and reports are stored locally on your phone. They are not uploaded to any servers or shared without your explicit action.

Can I export or share the inspection results?

Yes. The app generates a complete PDF report that can be saved or shared directly from your device.

Is the checklist customizable?

Yes. You can add your own custom checklist items and notes in addition to the predefined inspection points.

Is this app affiliated with Tesla?

No. Sjekkliste_Y is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Tesla, Inc.
