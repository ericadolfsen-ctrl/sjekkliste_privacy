What data is collected?

Sjekkliste_Y does not collect personal data on any server.

Information you add in the app may include notes and photos connected to checklist items. This content is stored locally on your device.

Storage and sharing

Storage: On your device

The app stores content locally. When you choose to share a PDF report, sharing happens through the iOS share sheet to services you select (e.g., Files, Mail, Messages).

Permissions

The app may request access to the camera and/or photos so you can document issues with images. You can change permissions at any time in iOS: Settings → Sjekkliste_Y.
