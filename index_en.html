Sjekkliste_Y

Sjekkliste_Y is a simple and focused app for inspecting your Tesla Model Y at delivery. The app helps you go through the car step by step, document issues with photos, and generate a complete PDF report directly on your phone.

Main features

  • Step-by-step checklist tailored for Tesla Model Y
  • Mark each item as OK or with an issue
  • Add notes and up to three photos per checklist item
  • Create your own custom checklist items
  • Verify that all required equipment is included
  • Generate a PDF report that can be saved or shared
